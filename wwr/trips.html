Let's get Wet and Wild!

Jungle Fever

Explore the majestic mountains and immerse yourself in nature. This thrilling adventure includes guided rafting, breathtaking views, and camping along the riverbank and encounter a range of different rapids, from mild to extreme depending upon the river. These trips can last anywhere from two to 21 days and cover dozens to hundreds of miles of river. Perfect for nature enthusiasts and thrill-seekers.

Day trips

Day trips are a great option for beginners and families. These trips usually last between three and five hours and cover a few miles of river. Most trips encounter mild rapids and have plenty of time to relax and enjoy the scenery but there are also challenging options available.

Adventurous Explosion

Adventure trips are for the most experienced and adventurous river rafters. These trips often feature Class IV and V rapids that require expert navigation and a high level of physical fitness. You’ll find both mental and physical challenge on these trips, and you’ll need to work closely with your fellow rafters.

Safety First!

White water rafting can be a thrilling and enjoyable adventure, but it’s important to take safety seriously. Here are some safety considerations:

  • Wear a life jacket at all times while on the water and a helmet if provided.
  • Listen during the safety orientation to know how to minimize your risk.
  • Stay hydrated and protected from the sun.
  • Always follow your guide’s instructions.
  • If you fall into the water rafting, try to stay on your back with your feet facing downstream to ward off rocks and avoid under water hazards.
